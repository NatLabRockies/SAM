IPH Linear Fresnel Direct Steam
===============================

The industrial process heat linear Fresnel direct steam model is similar to the :doc:`CSP linear Fresnel direct steam <../csp-linear-fresnel-direct-steam/dslf_overview>` model, but assumes that heat from the solar field is used for a thermal application rather than to drive a power cycle for electricity generation. It is for a solar field of linear Fresnel concentrating collectors.

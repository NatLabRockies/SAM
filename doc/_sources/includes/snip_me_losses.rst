
The inputs on the Losses page account for potential sources of energy loss that area not accounted for by the marine energy performance model.

The default 2% transmission loss and a 5% array/device downtime loss are based on the `reference model project <https://energy.sandia.gov/energy/renewable-energy/water-power/technology-development/reference-model-project-rmp/>`__. 

**Total Losses**
  SAM applies the total loss to the annual energy production of the system. The total loss percentage is the sum of the different loss categories.

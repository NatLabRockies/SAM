Summary
=======

The **Summary** tab displays the Metrics table with a selection of :doc:`results <../getting-started/results_page>` for each case in the project file and a selection of graphs.

Use the :doc:`Data tables <data>` tab to list all of the case's results, and :doc:`Graphs <graphs>` tab to create more graphs of results.

Metrics Table
~~~~~~~~~~~~~

For a list and descriptions of the variables in the Metrics table, see

* :doc:`Financial Metrics <../financial-metrics/mtf_overview>` for metrics from the financial models, such as :doc:`levelized cost of energy (LCOE) <../financial-metrics/mtf_lcoe>`, :doc:`levelized PPA price <../financial-metrics/mtf_lppa>`, :doc:`payback period <../financial-metrics/mtf_payback>`, :doc:`IRR <../financial-metrics/mtf_irr>`, etc..

* :doc:`Performance Metrics <../performance-metrics/mtp_overview>` for metrics from the performance models, such as :doc:`annual energy <../performance-metrics/mtp_annual_energy>`, :doc:`capacity factor <../performance-metrics/mtp_capacity_factor>`, etc.

The Metrics table shows financial model results from the :doc:`Cash flow <cashflow>` tab and performance model results. For example, this Metrics table shows results for the detailed photovoltaic performance model and single owner financial model:

.. image:: ../images/SS_MetricsTable.png
   :align: center
   :alt: SS_MetricsTable.png

You can :doc:`export data <../reference/export_data>` from the Metrics table by right-clicking it (control-click if your mouse has one button) and choosing an option, **Copy table** to copy the data to your computer's clipboard, or **Copy as CSV** to export it to a comma-separated text file:

.. image:: ../images/SS_MetricsTableRightClick.png
   :align: center
   :alt: SS_MetricsTableRightClick.png

Summary Graphs
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

The summary graphs are the graphs on the Summary tab. You can create your own graphs on the :doc:`Graphs <graphs>` tab.

To :doc:`export data or an image <../reference/export_data>` of a graph, right-click it (control-click on a mouse with one button) and choose an option from the shortcut menu.

To move a summary graph:

* Use your mouse to hover over an edge of the graph until the pointer changes to |SS_Pointer-Move| and then drag the graph to a new position. You may need to resize other graphs to make room for the graph.

.. image:: ../images/SS_Summary-MoveGraph.png
   :align: center
   :alt: SS_Summary-MoveGraph.png

To resize a summary graph:

* Use your mouse to hover over a corner of the graph until the pointer changes to |SS_Pointer-Resize| and then drag the corner of the graph to make it larger or smaller.

.. image:: ../images/SS_Summary-ResizeGraph.png
   :align: center
   :alt: SS_Summary-ResizeGraph.png

To reposition the legend in a graph:

* Click the legend and drag it to a new position.

.. |SS_Pointer-Resize| image:: ../images/SS_Pointer-Resize.png
.. |SS_Pointer-Move| image:: ../images/SS_Pointer-Move.png

p90 Energy
==========

For the Wind Power model, when you enable the uncertainty model on the :doc:`Uncertainties <../wind-power/wind_uncertainties>` input page, the p90 Energy metric is the p90 value of the wind farm's annual electricity output. The :doc:`Uncertainties <../results/uncertainties>` tab on the Results page displays a table of pXX values along with distribution plots of wind speed and annual energy probabilities.

Electricity Rates and Load
==========================

For projects with the :ref:`residential or commercial <rescom>` or :ref:`third party ownership <thirdparty>` financial model that buy and sell electricity at retail rates, you specify a rate structure (net metering, energy rates, demand charges, and monthly charges) on the :doc:`Electricity Rates <electricity_rates>` page, and a building or facility load on the :doc:`Electric Load <electricity_load>` page.

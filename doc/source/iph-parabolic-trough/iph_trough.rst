IPH Parabolic Trough
====================

The industrial process heat parabolic trough model is similar to the :doc:`CSP physical trough <../csp-physical-trough-model/troughphysical_overview>` model, but assumes that heat from the solar field is used for a thermal application rather than to drive a power cycle for electricity generation. It is for a solar field of parabolic trough concentrating collectors.

**Annual Developer (Lessee LLC) Margin (Sale Leaseback only)**
*Annual Developer Margin in Year n = Developer Operating Margin × (1 + Developer Margin Escalation)^(n-1)*

  Where *Developer Operating Margin*   and *Developer Margin Escalation*   are from the :doc:`Financing <../financial-parameters/fin_sale_leaseback>`   page.

**Total operating expense**
  The total operating expense is the sum of expenses:

*Total Operating Expense in Year n = Fixed Operating Expense + Capacity-based Operating Expense + Production-based Operating Expense + Fuel Expense + Insurance Expense + Property Tax Expense*

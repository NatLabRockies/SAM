
Energy Values for Financial Models Except Single Owner
------------------------------------------------------

**Energy, kWh**
  For systems that generate electricity, the total amount of electricity generated by the system in AC kilowatt-hours for each year.

  For solar water heating systems, the total amount of electricity saved by the solar water heating system in AC kilowatt-hours for each year.

Energy Values for the Single Owner Financial Model
--------------------------------------------------

**Energy to grid, kWh**
  Total AC electricity delivered to the grid by the power system in each year of the analysis period, including electricity discharged by the battery if applicable.

  This value is the sum of the hourly or subhourly Electricity to grid values.

**Energy from grid, kWh**
  Total grid AC electricity consumed by the power system in each year to meet loads for inverter night-time consumption, battery charging, and freeze protection and other parasitic loads for concentrating power systems.

  This value is the sum of hourly or subhourly Electricity from grid values.

**Net energy to grid, kWh**
  The total net AC electricity delivered to the grid by the power system in each year of the analysis period. For projects with a fixed power price, this is the value used to determine project revenue. For projects with time-of-delivery (TOD) power price multipliers, the revenue is calculated on a time-step basis.

Energy and Degradation
----------------------

For Year One of the project cash flow, the energy values are calculated the performance model

*Energy in Year One = Sum of Hourly or Sub-hourly Values*

Where Sum of Hourly or Sub-hourly Values is the system's total annual electrical output (or energy saved), equal to the sum of the values calculated by the performance model (8,760 values for hourly simulations).

For Years 2 and later:

*Energy in Year n>1 = Energy in Year n-1 × (1 - degradation rate)*

Where Energy in Year n-1 is the previous year's energy value and degradation rate is from the :doc:`Degradation <../degradation/degradation>` page.

.. note:: If you specified **degradation rate** on the :doc:`Degradation <../degradation/degradation>` page using the :doc:`annual schedule <../window-reference/win_edit_data_table_column>` option, SAM applies the value as follows:

.. note:: *Energy in Year n>1 = Energy in Year 1 × ( 1 - degradation rate ÷ 100% )*


The insurance cost applies in Year One and later of the cash flow:

*Insurance in Year n = Total Installed Costs ($) × Insurance (%) × (1 + Inflation Rate)^(n-1)*

Where *Total Installed Costs* is the value from the :doc:`Installation Costs <../installation-costs/index>`   page, and Insurance and Inflation Rate are specified on the :doc:`Financial Parameters <../financial-parameters/fin_overview>` page.

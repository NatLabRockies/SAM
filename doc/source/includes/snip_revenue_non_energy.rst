
Non-energy revenues allow for modeling of projects that combine revenue from non-energy sources, such as agricultural revenue through agrivoltaics.

**Gross non-energy revenue, $/year**
  Annual non-energy revenues that are applied to the cash flow.

**Gross non-energy expenses, $/year**
  Annual non-energy expenses that are applied to the cash flow.

**Non-energy revenue/expenses retained by energy owner, %**
  Percent of non-energy revenue or expenses that are applied to the cash flow. This input allows the user to specify revenue sharing agreements (for example where 5% of the crop revenue is retained by the solar owner in each year.)
  
**Energy revenue/expenses retained by energy owner, %**
  Percent of energy revenue or expenses that are applied to the cash flow. This includes PPA revenue, capacity payments, and curtailment payments, but not production based incentives. This input allows the user to specify revenue sharing agreements (for example where 95% of the energy revenue is retained by the solar owner in each year.)
  
**Escalation rate, %/yr**
  For non-energy revenue and expenses, you can specify an optional annual **Escalation Rate** to represent an expected annual increase or decrease in operating cost above the annual inflation rate specified on the :doc:`Financial Parameters <../financial-parameters/fin_overview>`   page.

  Specify an escalation rate of zero for an operating cost that increases annually at the rate of inflation, a positive escalation rate for a cost that increases at a higher rate than the inflation rate, or a negative escalation rate for a cost that increases at a lower rate than the inflation rate. (Set the inflation rate to the negative value of the inflation rate for a cost that does not increase with inflation.)
